Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 September 2025

1. Who we are

Folks Hub (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a social‑matching service that helps people connect and chat...

2. Information we collect

  • Account data – email address, password (hashed), Firebase UID.
  • Profile data – photos, age, gender, city & country, etc.
  • Usage data – swipes, likes, favourites, messages sent/received.
  • Device data – IP address, device model, OS version, etc.
  • Push‑notification token (FCM) so we can deliver match alerts.

3. How we use your information

  • Display your profile to others and match you accordingly.
  • Enable core features like chat and favorites.
  • Detect spam and secure accounts.
  • Show ads using Google AdMob based on your consent.
  • Improve product features via Firebase analytics.

4. Legal bases

We process data under contract, legitimate interest, or consent as applicable.

5. Sharing & third‑party services

Partner Purpose Data Shared
Firebase Auth, profiles, chats Account/profile
AdMob Ads Device & ad ID
Google Play Services Anti-fraud Device verdict

6. Data retention

  • Profiles: until deleted or 24 months inactive.
  • Logs: up to 12 months.
  • Ad data: per Google (usually 13 months).

7. Your rights

  • Edit your profile anytime in the app.
  • Delete your account via the app.
  • Email us to request delete data.

8. Children

Folks Hub is for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Security

We use HTTPS, Google Cloud, and Firebase App Check to protect your data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Major changes may be announced in-app or by email.

11. Contact us
