Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your Folks Hub account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

  • Send an email to: g.a.t.folkshub.developers@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include your registered email or user ID

What will be deleted

  • Profile information
  • Photos
  • Matches and interactions
  • Account data stored in our database

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 7–30 days depending on backups and legal requirements.
